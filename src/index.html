"Hey! Where the hell are you??"

We needed a way to find friends in a crowd.

Why isn't there a better way to find someone in Santos Populares, or to let your mates to know your exact location, rather then endless phone calls, not so awsome directions, and shouting? Well, there is now.
Checking which friends are in Santos, where they are and how to get to them, that's what this app is all about.

How does it work?

inSantos is here to light your way to every friend who is in the same city as you, partying in the same neighborhood, but fate is not enough for your paths to cross. You can turn things interactive! You can allow others to know where you are, and keep track of every friend in town.

Ask your Facebook friends to install the app
Share your location and have your friends share theirs
Find out where everyone is!


In no time, find out which Facebook friends are currently in Santos, where, and how to get there.

inSantos works connecting through Facebook to find out which friends have the inSantos app installed.

It's very easy to add and remove friends from the list of people who can see your location, or go on complete ninja mode!

How did we make it?

inSantos is a Hackfridays product.
Every week at cloudoki, our Friday afternoons are reserved time to hacking whatever our developers finds interesting. The goal is to experiment technology, hack things and ideally create something fun and usefull. Like inSantos.

In the heart of the Lisbon, in the middle of Bairro Alto, and surrounded by a great team. This is the perfect scenario for experiments and creativity.

Five HackFridays, two offices and too many pizzas later, we came up with an App.
